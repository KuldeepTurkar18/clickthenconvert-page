Your web browser may be configured to accept cookies, and if it is, we may collect non-personally identifiable information using “cookies” and/or “tags”.

What’s a cookie you ask? Well we wish it was chocolate chip

”Cookies” are tiny files that can be placed on your device’s browser to identify the activities of your device on the klientboost.com website and other websites.

What’s a tag you ask?

”Tags”, also known as page tags, web beacons, or gif tags, help track website or email activities like how many times a specific page or email has been interacted with. The klientboost.com website, ads, or email sent on our behalf, may contain these types of tags.

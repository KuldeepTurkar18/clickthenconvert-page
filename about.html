Every Superhero Has a Kickass Orgin Story

Steve, our founder isn’t a superhero but the origin story is pretty cool. Steve’s tech background started in telecommunications. He started an SEO company in 2008 and grew to over 500 customers the first year. Steve has worked with countless companies as a consultant, partner, media buyer, product owner, project manager, and marketer of many many channels.

Today Steve friends know him as “The PPC Guy”. Steve has run campaigns as a media buyer across over 200 different clients ranging in spend from $10 a day to $300,000 a day (yes a day).

He ran media for clients cumulatively to the tune of $19 Million in advertising spend. With a 5x average ROAS producing over $67 Million in revenue between 2015 and 2021.

Steve is obsessed with data and because of that, here at Click Then Convert, we make decisions based on hundreds of successful campaigns and tested strategies that are not based on premonitions or “gut instincts.” They’re rooted in tens of thousands of hours of trial and error and backing up decisions with real data-driven insights.

Performance Marketing Analyst

Job Description
  • Review data to determine trends, themes, and overall brand health from a reputation standpoint - provide actionable insights for improving customer perception
  • Pull data from dashboards as needed
  • Review survey results and provide actionable insights
  • Assist in the creation of PowerPoint decks for leadership
  • Project manage various aspects of the research and data collection process
  • Communicate with third party vendors and internal stakeholders on data collection and findings
Role Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree
  • 3+ years of performance marketing, data analyst, brand health, reputation management experience
  • High business communication skills
  • Knowledge of Marketing Research techniques and prior experience with Brand Health/Purchase Funnel/Brand Equity tracking programs highly preferred
  • Expert knowledge of Excel - you should be able to sort, filter, navigate the data
  • Experience conducting data accuracy checks - able to determine was is statistically relevant
